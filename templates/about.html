A Brief Introduction

Hello and welcome to our site!


This is an interactive web-application revolving around visualisations and data exploration. We created this product during one of our courses, studying Computer Science and Engineering at Eindhoven University of Technology. The project was done following the scrum-method. We learned a lot from the process of building the product from scratch and hopefully this valuable experience will assist us with our futures endeavours.
